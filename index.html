What next?

  1. figure out your expenses using a tool like mint or personalcapital
  2. understand any debt and have a plan to pay it off
  3. set up a high yield savings account
  4. start an IRA if you haven't already ($6000 limit for 2020)
  5. consolidate any previous employer 401k accounts
  6. find ways to diversify your income streams!
